Why credits, not subscriptions

  • No one wants to pay monthly for something on their wall
  • Credits feel fair — pay for what you use
  • "Buy 10 more when I need them" is a comfortable mental model
  • Subscription available for those who want it — but optional

Lower friction. Happier customers. Still profitable.
